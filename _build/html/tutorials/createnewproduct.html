Create a New Product¶

The system allows effective management of products in the system. Products can be created, deleted, and edited.

How to do: Create New Products¶

To do this, follow these steps:


  1. Click All from the top right.


  1. Hover over the “+” icon and select the desired product (in this demo, we select Single Product).




  1. Enter the necessary information. Items with red marks are required to be filled out.


  1. Click Add Taxonomy to add taxonomy to the product.


  1. Taxonomy menu appears. Select the desired taxonomy (in this demo, we select Tops).




  1. View the selected taxonomy.


  1. Click Relationships tab to add an image to the product.


  1. Click on “+” to add an entity.


  1. Click the select button to add the desired image (button turns to blue when selected), then click Add.




  1. Drag the selected image to the right pane can also be performed to add the image.


  1. To add more image, click Return, and repeat the previous steps.
