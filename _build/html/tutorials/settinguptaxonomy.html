Set Up a Taxonomy¶

Taxonomy is a product classification used to organize the content.

How to do: Set Up a Taxonomy¶

To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Click Settings.


  1. Click Data Model tab.


  1. Click Taxonomies to expand.


  1. Then, click Master Taxonomy.


  1. Use the “+” to expand the taxonomy, vertically or horizontally.


  1. Taxonomy window appears. Enter details for the new taxonomy and click Create.




Icons can also be added to the Taxonomy.

  1. Click a section under taxonomies (in this demo, we select Tops).


  1. Click upload icon and select an image from your computer.


  1. View the selected icon.
