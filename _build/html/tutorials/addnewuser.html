Add New Users¶

Manage different roles or profiles within the system by adding new users.

How to do: Add New Users¶

To do this, follow these steps:


  1. Click Settings.


  1. Click Partner Admin.


  1. Click Users on the left sidebar.


  1. Then, click “+” to add a user.


  1. Enter data in the fields.


  1. Then, click Create.


  1. View the added user.


Icon for the user can also be added. To do this, follow the steps below:

  1. Click Add Icon and select an image icon from your computer.


  1. View the added icon.
