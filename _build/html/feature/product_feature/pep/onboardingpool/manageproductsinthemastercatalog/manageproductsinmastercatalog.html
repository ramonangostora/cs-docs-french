Manage Products in the Master CatalogÂ¶

The Manage Products in the Master Catalog is a feature that allows a Data Steward to add information to the products so that the products have complete definitions.

Description:
  • Master Catalog allows a Data Steward to manage all products at the time of on-boarding.
  • The feature also allows a Data Steward to manage all the content and data of a product before transferring it to PIM.
Related topics:
  • On-boarding Features
  • Users and Permissions Features
Application:

  • The Master Catalog feature can permit users to manage all of the product content.

  • Example:

    An organization has a Data Steward who manages all the products in the Master Catalog and there are Suppliers A and B, who sell furniture and fashion products. Data Steward gives permission for Data Integration to both suppliers. They proceeded with on-boarding of their products and transferring to the Master Catalog, where all the Products are verified and corrected by the Data Steward, if necessary.
