Manage Prepacks¶

Manage Prepacks is a feature that allows a user to group together different products to create Prepacks, which are assembled offerings of individual products in various quantities that can be ordered by stores.

Description:
  • It is a collection of single articles (similar or dissimilar), tied or wrapped up together as one.
  • This feature creates Threshold Bundle, which is also known as Loose bundle.
Related topics:
  • Manage Displays
Application:

  • A Loose bundle or Combination of Products may help retailers to increase the sales of products.

  • This type of bundle can be used during stock clearance, where the retailer can add a single item in multiple quantities or can sell the bundle at a discounted price.

  • Example:

    A Retailer puts together a prepack of 3 shampoos for 80 bucks or a prepack of shampoo, conditioner, and face wash for 100 bucks. These products can be sold either as a combo or individual items.

How to do: Manage Prepacks¶

To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Click Administration icon.


  1. Click Data Model tab.Then, click Article


  1. Click on the ‘+’ icon to create the type of product.


  1. Fill in the required details and enable the tab for nature class type.


  1. click Type to select from the dropdown list and select Threshold. Click CREATE to continue.


  1. In the content window of the class, enable Default. Remember to mention Maximum Number of products you would like to add. Save the changes.


  1. Similarly, there are various fields where you can add Events, Embedded Classes etc.


  1. Add property collection in the Section tab by clicking on ‘+’ icon and click Apply.






  1. In the Relationship section, select the class by clicking on Other Side (in thios demo, we selected ‘article’). The type of class you choose here will decide what kind of products you can add to threshold bundles.


  1. Next, click on the Explore tab. Hover to the ‘+’ icon to see ‘Loose Bundle’ type of class that was created earlier.






  1. Next, Click on the class Loose Bundle.


  1. Click on Overview tab to find a sub-tab Bundle Section.


  1. Click + icon, which opens up to another window, where list of products you made are visible.


  1. Select the product by clicking on checkbox, and drag it to the right hand side of the space provided.


  1. After selecting products, click on the Add Product icon.




  1. Click Back to add more products. Images can also be added in the Relationship tab.
