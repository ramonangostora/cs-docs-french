Manage Individual Articles¶

Manage Individual Articles enables you to manage a product at the level of a Single Article. Any attribute, relationship or classification can be managed at the level of an individual Single Article.

Description:
  • A Single Article is the basic unit in Contentsphere for a product without variants.
  • Contentsphere allows you to create, edit and delete individual products.
Related topics:
  • Manage Units for individual articles
Application:

  • Create a Single Article in the system to represent a single item.

  • Example:

    Let’s say a retailer wants to add a new flavor of Red Bull to their stock. To do that, they needs to create this product as a Single Article.

How to do: Manage Individual Articles¶

Managing individual articles is easy, and here’s how to do it.


  1. Go to PIM.


  1. Hover on the “+” icon.


  1. Select Single Article.


  1. Enter the name for the single article and provide relevant information.


  1. Click the Relationships button.


  1. Click the “+” icon under Images.


  1. Select the image and click the Add All button (or drag and drop the image to the right side).


  1. Click the Back button to check that the image is added to the product.



  1. Add markets. The steps are the same as with adding images except that you click the “+” icon under Market.





  1. Check your single article.
